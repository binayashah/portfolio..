My Chorchet Projects

I love working on chrochet projects because they allow me to express my creativity while making something beautiful and useful.Chrochet has become a relaxing hobby for me. It’s always rewarding to see a project come to life, one loop at a time.
Here are some details about my works.

Bunny Doll

I made a cute crochet bunny doll using soft yarn and simple stitches.This project helped me practice shaping and stitching.

Silam Sakma

One of my special projects is making Silam Sakma, a traditional badge worn by the Rai and Limbu communities.

Chorchet Tulips

I enjoy making crochet tulips using bright and colorful yarn. They look like real flowers and never fade, making them perfect for home decoration.

Amigrumi Keyring
